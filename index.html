Sign Lens

About Sign Lens

Sign Lens is a tool which is based on a computer vision system that can recognize and detect alphanumeric characters in real-time. The system is designed to detect and recognize characters from persons gestures in various lighting conditions and environments

How It works

The system is designed to take input from a camera and process the video stream in real-time. The output of the system will be the recognized alphanumeric characters

Goal

The goal is to become a robust and reliable system for Indian sign detection that can be deployed in a variety of applications. The system's output will be used to provide valuable insights.
